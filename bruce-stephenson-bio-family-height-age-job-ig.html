Austin's Bruce Stephenson Bio, Family, Height, Age, IG

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Meet Bruce Stephenson, one of eight casts on Netflix’s new show “Twentysomethings: Austin.” Here you’ll learn about his age, his job, his IG, and his family.

So, tag along with this article to learn more about him.

Bruce Stephenson On Twentysomethings: Austin

Bruce is a Rom-Com watching contestant of Netflix’s new show “Twentysomethings: Austin”. He hailed from Greenville, South Carolina. After losing his job, he moved back with his parents and lent hand to his insurance business. But, he explained that instance isn’t his calling.

In the trailer of the show he explained, “I heard Austin is a young, bustling town, and me being 23, that’s something that I want to be a part of.” He is joined by Kamari Bonds, Abbey Humphries, Raquel Daniels, Natalie Gable, Isha Punja, Keauno Perez, and Michael Fractor are among the cast members joining Bruce Stephenson.

Bruce promoted his appearance on his IG where he shared that he was thankful for the opportunity and grateful towards his parents and siblings.

Is Bruce Stephenson Dating Anyone?

His Facebook states that Bruce Stephenson is single. He was close with one Millie Drake since high school. They even went to prom together and were “king n’ queen 2k16.” In April 2020, Bruce wrote on his Instagram that everyone thinks they are a couple but they are just a couple of friends.

How Old Is Bruce Stephenson?

Bruce Stephenson is 23 years old as of 2021.

Bruce Stephenson Family

Born to Harry Stephenson and Kelly Stephenson, he is allegedly one of the three kids born to his parents.

His father is the owner of Stephenson Insurance which he started in July 1993 in Greenville, North Carolina. His father earned his bachelor’s from Christ Church Episcopal, The Citadel in Business Administration and Management.

Not much is known about his mother who goes by the name (@mamakelly_2) on her Instagram which is private.

Talking about siblings, Bruce has a brother named Hal Stephenson who is a third mate at the International Organization of Masters, Mates & Pilots. He started his job in July 2021. Learn more about his education her other jobs here. He has featured his brother on his IG and wrote, “Proud of you bro.”

Also, Bruce has a sister named Lillian Scot. She is married and works as a Registered Nurse. She studied Nursing at USC Upstate Mary Black School of Nursing and Sociology at Wofford College. Her Facebook account is (@scots22).

Bruce Stephenson Job

Bruce Stephenson is currently working as Insurance Agent for his father’s company Stephenson Insurance. He started the job in September 2020. However, he started his career as Social Media Marketing Manager for EnVerias Group. He joined the company in June 2017 and worked until August 2017.

Before he left his job, Bruce had already started working as Sales Associate for his father’s company. He worked there from May 2017 to December 2018. From May 2018 – August 2018, he served YMCA of Greenville as a Member Services Specialist.

Bruce also worked for Daytrip where he served as Head Sales Intern for four months from May 2019-August 2019. He worked for Atlanta Braves in Retail Operations from January 2020 – June 2020 before he started his career as Insurance Agent.

Indeed.com, based on the data they collected revealed that Insurance Agent makes nearly $60,476 per annum.

Exploring details on his education, Bruce enrolled at Greenville Senior High Academy and graduated in 2016. While in high school he was on the varsity baseball team. He had to let go of his dream to become a baseball player because of his injury in 2015. It was the time he hit his first home run.

After that, Bruce enrolled at Clemon University and earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Science in Marketing. He graduated in 2019. In college, he served as New Member Educator and later became President of BYX-Beta Upsilon Chi-Brothers Under Christ until December 2019.

Other than that, Bruce also dabbled in theatre acting. He played pirate on “How I Became a Pirate” a South Carolina Children’s Theater musical based on local author Melinda Long’s best-selling book, first opened at the Peace Center in 2010. As the youngest cast member in the musical and scored the lead role of Jeremy Jacob, a young boy who joins a pirate crew. Learn more about theatrical works here.
