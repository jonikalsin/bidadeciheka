Silas Randall Timberlake- Meet Eldest Son Of Musician Justin Timberlake and His Actress Wife, Jessic

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 14, 2024

Silas Randall Timberlake is the eldest son of Hollywood stars, Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel. American singer-songwriter and record producer, Justin has sold over  88 million records worldwide, making him the best-selling music artist in the world. 

Born and raised in Tennessee, he has won various awards such as ten Grammy Awards, nine Billboard Music Awards, four Emmy Awards, and more. On the other hand, Jessica is an actress who is famous for her role as Mary Camden in the family drama series 7th Heaven.

Here is everything you need to know about Silas' family life.

Silas' grandparents are Randall Timberlake, Kimberly Biel, Jonathan Edward Biel, and Lynn Bomar Harless.

Parents' (Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel) Are Married Since 2012

As of now, Justin and Jessica have been in a marital relationship for nine years. The actor and actress who first met in December 2007 at a party, are known as one of the most powerful couples in Hollywood. 

Not long after they met, the smooching couple made their relationship public after the Daily Mail shared their first kissing photos in the United Kingdom for his world tour. After the, they became engaged during the trip to Montana in December 2011.

After about four years of on-again, off-again dating, Biel and Timberlake exchanged the vows on October 19, 2012, in a romantic $6.5 million ceremony in Italy. The bride was wearing the custom-made Haute Couture pink gown designed by Giambattista Valli.

100 guests attended their function like Jimmy Fallon, Timbaland, Andy Samberg, and Beverly Mitchell. Justin was accused of cheating on Jessica after he was photographed holding hands with his Palmer co-star Alisha Wainwright in New Orleans in November 2019. 

He apologized to his family a few weeks later.

Similarly, Jessica was in a relationship with her 7th Heaven castmate Adam LaVorgna from 1998 to 2001. They had their first interaction on the set of I'll Be Home for Christmas. Also, she hooked up with actor Chris Evans for five years from 2002 to 2006.
