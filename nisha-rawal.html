Nisha Rawal is an Indian television actress, model, social media personality, YouTuber, singer, dancer, and theatre artist. She has been cast in a variety of projects which include Main Lakshmi Tere Aangan Ki, Shaadi Mubarak, Aane Wala Pal, Kesar, Hastey Hastey, Nach Baliye 5, Nach Baliye Shriman v/s Shrimati, Rafoo Chakkar, and Jack N Jhol.

Boyfriend / Spouse

Nisha Rawal has dated –

  • Karan Mehra (2006-2021) – She started dating actor, model, and fashion designer Karan Mehra in the year 2006, after he saw her on the sets of Hastey Hastey where he was styling. After dating for a few years, they got married on November 24, 2012. Unfortunately, she suffered a miscarriage in 2014. Later, Nisha found out that she was again pregnant while Karan was in the Bigg Boss house and they welcomed their first child, a boy named Kavish Mehra, together in 2017. However, their marriage hit rock bottom in 2021 when Nisha accused Karan Mehra of domestic violence and Karan was then arrested.
