Personal Life of Alka Yagnik

  • Yagnik was born in a well-royal Gujarati family on 20 March 1966 in Kolkata, West Bengal, she was a brilliant student in her school days but she did not like studies.
  • In her childhood, she was fascinated with singing as a result, she started her singing career in 1972 when she was just 6 years old in All India Radio Calcutta.
  • Alka’s mother supported her singing passion by shifting to Mumbai for her career growth and better singing opportunities.
  • Her mother Shubha Yagnik also a singer of Indian Classical Music and interestingly Alka got her singing skills from her mother.
  • Yagnik got her first singing training from her mother and She considered her mother as her first Guru, later she took training under the famous singer Kalyanji-Anandji and Laxmikant-Pyarelal.
  • She has sung songs in many genres but she was excited and suitable with romantic genres due to which she sang mostly romantic songs in her singing career.

Alka was married to Shillong-based businessman Neeraj Kapoor in 1989, the couple together had a daughter Syesha Kapoor.
