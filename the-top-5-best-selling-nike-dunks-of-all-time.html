The Top 5 Best Selling Nike Dunks of All Time

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 1, 2024

Nike Dunks have seen a surge in popularity over the years, becoming a cultural phenomenon and a staple in the sneaker community. With various iconic collaborations and colorways, some Nike Dunks have stood out as best sellers. Let's delve into the top 5 best selling Nike Dunks of all time based on their impact and desirability.

1. Travis Scott x Nike SB Dunk Low Cactus Jack, 2020

Travis Scott's collaboration with Nike on the SB Dunk Low Cactus Jack in 2020 sent sneakerheads into a frenzy. The earthy color scheme, rope-style laces, woodsy camo pattern, and Cactus Jack branding perfectly embodied the rapper's aesthetic, making this Dunk one of the most hyped releases. The overwhelming demand even led to the temporary shutdown of the Nike website, solidifying its status as a best seller.

2. Ben & Jerry’s x Nike SB Chunky Dunky, 2020

The collaboration between Nike and Ben & Jerry's resulted in the Chunky Dunky, a sneaker that captured the attention of sneakerheads worldwide. Despite the challenges of a pandemic and economic uncertainties, this release showcased the unwavering passion of sneaker enthusiasts. The incorporation of cow print leather overlays, tie-dye collar, and Ben & Jerry’s logo on the upper made this Dunk an instant classic, contributing to its remarkable sales.

3. Union LA x Nike Dunk Low Argon, 2022

Although not technically an SB Dunk, the Union LA x Nike Dunk Low Argon from 2022 stands out as one of the most sought-after Dunks. This collaboration with LA retailer UNION captured the essence of the Dunk's legacy and contributed to its immense popularity. Despite not being labeled as an SB Dunk, its significance in the Dunk lineage and the fervor surrounding its release make it a best seller.

4. Nike SB Dunk De La Soul, 2005

Paying tribute to the legendary hip-hop group De La Soul, the Nike SB Dunk De La Soul from 2005 holds a special place in the hearts of sneaker enthusiasts. With a color scheme inspired by the group's iconic debut album, "3 Feet High and Rising," this Dunk resonated with hardcore De La Soul fans and sneaker collectors alike. The unique design elements and historical significance of this release contributed to its exceptional sales and enduring popularity.

5. Stussy x Nike SB Dunk Cherry, 2005

The collaboration between Stüssy and Nike resulted in the SB Dunk Cherry, a fan favorite known for its Neapolitan Ice Cream-inspired colorway. Despite the mystery behind the addition of a cherry to the tongue, this Dunk's unique color scheme and Stüssy's influence made it a top seller. The unconventional yet fresh design elements, coupled with Stüssy's cultural impact, solidified the Dunk Cherry's status as one of the best selling Nike Dunks of all time.

These five Nike Dunks have left an indelible mark on sneaker culture, captivating enthusiasts and collectors alike. Their unique designs, influential collaborations, and cultural significance have propelled them to the top of the best sellers list, cementing their status as iconic footwear in the sneaker community.
