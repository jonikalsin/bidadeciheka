Who Is Cookie Challenge's Lucie Radcliffe? Her Wiki, Age, Married, Height

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 28, 2024

Food Network’s Christmas Cookie Challenge officially returned for its 5th season as the festive season was slowly kicking in. The show premiered on Food Network on the 11th of November 2021 wherein the viewers got to see five former Christmas Cookie Challenge champions return to see how good they fare against the new set of contestants. Among the returning contestants was a former Christmas Cookie Challenge champion, Lucie Radcliffe.

In this article, we will talk about her chances to be the “Best of the Best” by winning Christmas Cookie Challenge for the second time. We will also talk about her family, job, married life, and more!

Meet Lucie Radcliffe, The Food Network’s Cookie Challenge Champion

For the first episode (5th Season) of The Food Network’s Christmas Cookie Challenge, all the contestants including Lucie had to form a Christmas village puzzle out of six perfect interlocking cookies. Their next challenge was to craft the tallest cookie display ever seen. Plus, they had to do all their creations using spices such as chili, paprika, coriander, and cloves.

Previously, Lucie had already appeared in the Cookie Challenge competition in October 2019. The filming of that season was already completed by the end of February 2019, Lucie revealed in her blog. Talking about whether or not she knew her competitors beforehand, she said, “Nope! But it was an awesome surprise when I found out I was competing against Pete from @petey_cake — we already knew each other over Instagram! Stephen, Lourdes, and Sarahkay were also all such lovely and talented people, and our group has kept in touch ever since.”

In her blog, Lucie also revealed that the show is not scripted or rigged. She told that all the challenges and time limits viewers saw were real.

Lucie Radcliffe Age

Lucie Radcliffe was born in the year 1989; her age was 32 as of November 2021. She celebrates her birthday on the 6th of March every year. Her zodiac sign is Pisces.

Lucie Radcliffe Family

To talk about Lucie Radcliffe’s family, we know that her mom’s name is Liz Radcliffe and she works as a realtor at RE/MAX Crest Realty Westside. For her undergraduate studies, Liz went to McMaster University and the University of British Columbia. Lucie’s mom did her high schooling at Little Flower Academy.

As of this writing, we don’t have any information on Lucie’s dad though. However, we do know that Lucie’s mom and dad are not together anymore as they divorced on July 15, 2016.

Lucie has three siblings, a brother, and two sisters. David Radcliffe is Lucie’s brother whereas Sophie and Emma are her sisters. David studied Psychology at the University of British Columbia and also went to Point Grey Secondary School for education.

Is Lucie Radcliffe Married?

If you are wondering if Lucie Radcliffe is a single or a married woman, well she is neither. As of November 2021, she is engaged to her boyfriend-turned-fiance, Spencer Steele (@spencersteele442).

In an Instagram post made on December 28, 2019, Lucie’s boyfriend, Spencer revealed that she accepted his proposal and they were finally engaged.

Lucie first appeared on Spencer’s Instagram on October 9, 2015. Although we don’t the exact date they began dating from, we suppose it’s around the same date when Lucie made her first appearance on Spencer’s Instagram.

The lovely duo celebrated their first dating anniversary on August 14, 2016. Spencer shared the milestone on his Instagram handle. Spencer also wished his girlfriend Valentine’s Day on February 14, 2017.

Lucie Radcliffe Career

Based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, Lucie Radcliffe is a graphic designer, sugar artist, and baker.

Lucie is a content creator and the owner of Lucie Bakes according to her LinkedIn profile. Her LinkedIn profile also tells us that she was a freelance graphic designer at Lucie Radcliffe Designer for 8 years. Furthermore, Lucie worked at SAGA Sciences from October 2015 to September 2019 as a graphic designer.

She also runs her website which goes as lucieradcliffe.com. There she shares her Livestream Classes, recipes, tutorials, workbooks, among many other things.

For education, Lucie went to Kwantlen Polytechnic University and completed her Bachelor of Design, Graphic Design for Marketing from there in 2013. She did her high schooling at Lord Byng Secondary School.
