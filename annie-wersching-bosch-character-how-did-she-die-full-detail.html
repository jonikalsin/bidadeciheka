Annie Wersching Career

Wersching made her acting debut on Star Trek: Enterprise, and has since appeared in guest parts on Charmed, Killer Instinct, Supernatural, and Cold Case, among others. She had a recurring role as Amelia Joffe in the ABC serial drama General Hospital from March 2007 to November 2007. She also had stints at the Utah Shakespearean Festival, the Marriott Lincolnshire, and the Victory Gardens.

In the seventh and eighth seasons of 24, Wersching portrayed FBI Special Agent Renee Walker. Wersching has appeared in numerous television episodes as a guest star since her departure from 24 after two seasons. These shows include CSI, NCIS, Rizzoli & Isles, Hawaii Five-0, Body of Proof, Dallas, Revolution, Castle, Blue Bloods, The Vampire Diaries, and Touch. The year 2009 saw Wersching take part in the Taco Bell All-Star Legends and Celebrity Softball Game.
