How Old Is The Businessman?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, May 13, 2024

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr wikipedia unfolds his successful business career and love life he lived with his wife, Charo Santos-Concio.

Charo Santos-Concio is a Filipino actress and media executive best known for hosting a Philippine television drama anthology, Maalaala Mo Kaya.

Born in October 1955 in Calapan, Oriental Mindoro, Charo graduated from St. Paul University Manila.

Furthermore, Santos-Concio served as a chief executive officer of ABS-CBN Corporation from 2012 to 2016. Now, she is a chief content officer of ABC-CBN University. 

Santos-Concio has produced and acted in many Filipino movies and TV series. She was happily married to her partner, Cesar Rafael Concio Jr. She is the mother of two children.

Besides her career, let’s talk about Santos-Concio’s partner, Cesar Rafael Concio Jr Wikipedia and age.

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr Wikipedia

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr. was a Businessman and a loving husband of Charo Santos-Concio.

Cesar was successful in his business. He was involved in the entertainment industry for a long time. He was also associated with Filsyn Corporation and Franmar Corporation.

According to Ateneo de Manila University, Cesar was associated with Ateneo De Manila University for a long time. He became a member of the University’s Board of Trustees in 1971.

Furthermore, Cesar served as the Chair of the Board of the University in 1972 for about nine years. He was also president of the Ateneo Alumni Association from 1971 to 1975.

He did not publicly speak up about himself like her wife, Charo Santos-Concio. Cesar had been married to Charo for over four decades. The pair met each other while they were working at the ABS-CBN.

As per the sources, Cesar was the president of ABS-CBN when the pair first met. Later, the pair began dating and got married in 1982.

However, Cesar and Charo have a long age gap but lived happily together for many years.

But sadly, Cesar Rafel Concio Jr died on 4 October 2023. His sudden demise was revealed on the website of Ateneo De Manila University. 

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr Age: How Old Was The Businessman?

As per the source, Cesar Rafael Concio Jr was born in 1931. He was 92 years old at the time of his demise.

Cesar was involved in entertainment, education and the news industry for a long time. 

Cesar’s dedication and hard work opened a platform for many students and became a field to learn and practice.

During his service in the ABS-CBN, he was known for his tough services and management of the company.

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr’s sudden demise has significantly affected his wife, children, colleagues and loved ones.

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr Love Life

Cesar Rafael Concio Jr and Charo Santos-Concio tied their wedding knot in 1982. They are also parents of their two children.

As per Philstar.com, Cesar was previously married and had children. But he was separated from his wife and children.

After meeting Charo in the entertainment industry, Cesar and Charo became good friends.

He invited Charo to join him and his friends to tour Europe at France’s 1982 Cannes Film Festival after the completion of their film Mike de Leon’s Kisapmata screening. 

Charo also needed a break from her acting career, and she agreed to join Cesar. While dining on the Europe tour, Charo asked Cesar if he had any plans to remarry again.

Later, Cesar replied that he was thinking of marrying Charo. As both liked each other a lot, they began dating and tied their wedding knot on 9 November 1982 in Pebble Beach, California.

Charlo often mentioned her husband in interviews, thanking him for his continuous love and support for her life. They also have a two son, Francis and Martin.

Overall, Cesar Rafael Consio Jr. was the loving partner of Charo Santos-Concio and the father of his two sons. His sudden loss has created a significant impact on the family.
