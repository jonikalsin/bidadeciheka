On April 3, 1992, Damien Williams, a running back for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League, was born. He participated in collegiate football at Oklahoma.

Along with playing for the Kansas City Chiefs, he also took part in Super Bowl IV. With less than two minutes left in that Super Bowl, he ran 38 yards for the game-winning touchdown for the Chiefs.

Williams joined the freshman class at El Cajon Valley High School and played varsity sports there. For the next three years, he participated in high school football at Mira Mesa Senior High School.
