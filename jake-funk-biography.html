Who is Jake Funk?

Jake Funk is a known American football running back. Jake Funk plays for the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League (NFL).

Los Angeles Ram drafted him to play college football at Georgia in the seventh round of the 2021 NFL Draft.

Currently, he is playing in the Super Bowl LVI 2022 as a football running back.

Jake Funk- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Jake Funk was born to his parents  Jim and A’Lisa Funk on September 8, 1992. His birthplace is Gaithersburg, Maryland, the United States.

Currently, Funk is 30 years old and has American nationality. His ethnicity is Caucasian whereas his birth zodiac sign is Capricorn.

He belongs to an athlete’s family. His father had been a football player who had played for Penn State while his mother was the national title-winner in swimmer at the Clarion University of Pennsylvania.
