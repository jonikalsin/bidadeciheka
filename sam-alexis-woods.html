Duk abin da kuke buatar sani game da 'yar Tiger Woods: Sam Alexis Woods

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Sam Alexis Woods ita ce 'yar wasan golf, Tiger Woods, kuma tsohuwar matar, Elin Nordegren ne adam wata. An haifi Sam Alexis sa'o'i 24 bayan mahaifinta ya ci nasara ta biyu ta zo ta biyu award a cikin 2007 US Open wasa.

Wasu da yawa sun tambayi ko sunan, Sam, gajere ne ga Samantha. Tiger ya shaida wa manema labarai duk da haka ya sanya mata suna Sam don girmama mahaifinsa marigayi (kakan Sam Alexis) tun lokacin da aka haife ta kwana guda bayan ranar Uba.

Sam Alexis yana da ƙane da ake kira Charlie Axel. Elin ita ce mahaifiyar Sam da Charlie. Tiger da Elin sun rabu yanzu, kuma suna hannun jarin ’ya’yansu. Elin ya raba wa manema labarai cewa Tiger ya ci gaba da zama uba mai ban sha'awa ga 'ya'yansu.

Shin Sam Alexis Woods kuma yana buga wasan golf?

Tiger ya tabbatar a cikin wata hira cewa ya sanya kulob din golf a hannun Sam makonni kadan bayan an haife ta. Tun tana girma itama tasan daddynta.

A wannan lokacin, ko da yake, Sam ya fi sha'awar ƙwallon ƙafa fiye da golf. Mahaifinta ya halarci wasannin ƙwallon ƙafa da dama da ayyukanta.

Wasu daga cikin abubuwan sha'awar Sam Alexis Woods

Baya ga ƙwallon ƙafa, Sam da ɗan'uwanta suna son kula da karnuka. Tiger ya saka hotunansu masu kyau kuma duk suna murmushi yayin wasa da cudanya da abokansu masu fusata.

Lokacin da ba ta buga ƙwallon ƙafa, ba ta daina kallon wasannin hukuma da kuma bin tauraro na Ƙungiyar Mata ta Amurka.

Shin akwai wasu mashahurai masu alaƙa da Sam Alexis Woods?

Bayan sanannen mahaifinta na golfer, Tiger Woods, Sam Alexis Woods kuma yana da alaƙa da sanannen golfer na LPGA Tour - Cheyenne dazuzzuka. 'Yan uwan ​​juna ne.

Menene asalin Sam Alexis Woods?

Sam Alexis Woods Ba'amurke ne.

Shekara nawa ne Sam Alexis Woods kuma nawa ne nauyinta?

An haifi 'yar Tiger Woods a ranar 18 ga Yuni, 2007. Za ta kasance shekaru 13 a watan Yuni 2020.

Babu wani bayani a hukumance kan nauyinta, tsayi, da ma'aunin jikinta a yanzu.

Menene darajar Sam Alexis Woods?

Sam ya kasance matashi kuma har yanzu yana makaranta. Har yanzu ba ta cikin wannan ma'aikata kuma ba ta da wani tushen samun kudin shiga na farko ko albashin shekara. Duk da haka, ƙimancin ƙima na Sam yana ƙunshe a ciki $ 1 Million to $ 5 Million. A matsayinta na ’yar shekara 13, iyayenta ne ke kula da kuɗin rayuwarta na shekara.
