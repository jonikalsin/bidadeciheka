Manbeers ugly reaction as Sahiba receive Regal Responsibility

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Present you the exclusive Teri Meri Doriyaann Written Update. A star Plus show with its gripping concept of romance, love triangle, bromance, sibling bond. Today’s Episode 241 depicts Akal and Jabjot’s faith on Sahiba. With its impeccable cast the show display true emotions of family values.

SEERAT’S CONDITION

In the recent events, Seerat gets traumatized and collect all the stuff of Garry. She hides sahiba’s gown. Seerat recall how garry use to treat her. She feels miserable. Angad enters seerat’s room. She tries to get close to Angad as seerat holds angad’s hand. Angad’s naiveness couldn’t understand seerat’s intensions. She told Angad you always supported me, I am glad for this. She put her head on angad’s arm. Sahiba wonders seerat should be okay today. Sahiba enters room, told seerat to visit mother-father to freshen up her mood. Seerat reply no you and sahiba both support me for staying in this house so I will live here.

DARJI- BEBE ORGANIZE FAMILY MEETING

Akal(Darji) and Jabjot(BeBe) starts speaking about sahiba’s loyalty towards Brar Family. Both assert there was a time when you and angad don’t choose each other, it is god’s grace that now you both cant live without each other. Bebe speak to giving responsibility of Brar Family to Sahiba. Everyone wonders and appreciate their decision except Manbeer. She assert it is my position how can you compare me with her, she belongs to low grade family.

Bebe stop Manbeer and explain it doesn’t matter from which family she belongs, she accepts us and take care of everyone. Inder supports sahiba, manbeer dislike this. Sahiba told everyone I am glad you thought about giving me this position but still I need to achieve few things, she signals on receiving love from Manbeer. Manbeer left the living room area.

INDER MANBEER FEUD

Manbeer assert to inder, cant you and angad support me for once. Inder reply you should be proud because you daughter in law achieve that level, now you should focus on your goals, dreams and live your life. Manbeer gets angry reply, you think now I am old that is why you go to gaytri, I am tolerating Simran that is enough for me.

PRECAP

Sahiba packs bag and left Brar Mansion. She returns Keys to Bebe. Sahiba assert you can ask angad why I am doing this.
