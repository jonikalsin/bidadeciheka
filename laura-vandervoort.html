Laura Vandervoort | Dating, Net Worth, Boyfriend, V-Wars, Smallville, Bitten, Supergirl, Husband

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 10, 2024

Laura Vandervoort was born on 22nd September 1984 in Toronto. Ontario. Her mother was Canadian while her father was from the Netherlands. She was diagnosed with meningitis within a few weeks of her birth. The doctors initially told her parents she wouldn’t live.

Laura was fond of several sports growing up, most notably football, karate, basketball, and tennis. She has one sibling, a sister named Sarah. The actress is related to the well-renowned Canadian actor Gordon Pinsent.

A Brief Look at the actress’ career – What is her net worth?

Laura Vandervoort holds an impressive net worth from her rising career.
Source: Laura Vandervoort Instagram

Vandervoort began her career when she was merely 13 years old. Initially performing as background characters in several commercials and TV shows, she received her first speaking role in the children series Goosebumps and Are You Afraid of the Dark?

Laura was studying Psychology and English at York University when she landed a starring role in the CTV teen drama Instant Star, playing the role of Sadie Harrison. The show aired for four seasons from 2004 to 2008.

Vandervoort’s next major role came in the series Smallville (2007-2011) where she played the character of Kara Zor-El for 23 episodes in seasons 7 (main role), 8 and 10 (guest appearance).

IGN Interview: Laura Vandervoort on Supergirl

From 2009 to 2011, the actress was a lead in the science fiction series V as Lisa for 22 episodes. In the next couple of years, she made brief appearances in Family Guy (2010-11), White Collar (2012), Finding Mrs. Claus (2012), Haven (2013), and CSI: NY (2013).

Laura played the main role of Elena Michaels in Bitten (2014-16). She then played recurring roles in Supergirl (2016) as Indigo / Brainiac 8 for 3 episodes, and Tessa in Ice (2016-18).

As for the big screens, the actress made her debut with the thriller film Troubled Waters (2006) as Carolyn. Her other notable movies include The Lookout (2007), The Jazzman (2009), The Entitled (2011), This Means War (2012), Ted (2012), Jigsaw (2017), and Rabid (2019).

Vandervoort’s net worth is estimated at $2.5 million.

Laura Vandervoort’s Dating History – Who is her current boyfriend?

Laura Vandervoort with her boyfriend Shawn Piller.

Laura Vandervoort is dating The Dead Zone co-creator, Shawn Piller. Her boyfriend, Shawn, is an executive producer at an independent content production company called Piller/Segan. The native of Los Angeles graduated from the University of Southern California.

The couple leaves no shortage of effort to display their affection for one another on social media. On 14th February 2019, Laura took to Instagram to express her love for her partner on Valentine’s Day. She wrote,

Happy Valentines to my ? My best friend and hopefully one day.... My business partner ? (inside joke) ?

The soon-to-be husband and wife duo is yet to welcome children into their life.

Past Affairs

Laura Vandervoort with her former boyfriend Corey Sevier.
Source: Ranker

Back in August 2010, Vandervoort was engaged to her Instant Star co-star Corey Sevier. The pair first met on the sets during the third season of the series. They subsequently began dating and did so for a while before striving towards engagement. Their relationship, however, failed to go the distance.

Laura then dated actor Chris Pines in 2011 after they bumped into each other while working together in the comedy spy film This Means War. A year later, she was in a relationship with another actor, Michael Rosenbaum, in October 2012. They first met way back in 2007 on the sets of Smallville. Their dating affair, however, lasted only five months as they split in February 2013.

Fans were certain Oliver Trevena will be Laura Vandervoort's husband.
Source: Pinterest

The Jigsaw star then had an affair with actor boyfriend Oliver Trevena. They dated from mid-2013 which led to an engagement on the final day of the same year. Oliver dropped his proposal during a vacation while they were visiting a castle in the UK. They made several public events together before deciding to separate in 2015.

Despite numerous disappointments in the past, the actress most likely carries no regrets as she eventually bumped into the love of her life in the form of Shawn Piller. The couple is together for a few years now. Fans tend to speculate a potential engagement or wedding could be on the cards.

Laura Vandervoort is starring in 'V-Wars' (2019)

The American science fiction horror series stars, V-Wars, is based on the comics by Jonathan Maberry. Laura Vandervoort plays the role of Mila Dubov in the series.

V-Wars stars notable cast, which includes Ian Somerhalder as Dr. Luther Swann, Kimberly-Sue Murray as Danika, Jacky Lai as Kaylee Vo, Kyle Breitkopf as Dez, Adrian Holmes as Michael Fayne, and Peter Outerbridge as Calix Niklos.

The series makes it premiere on 5th December 2019.
